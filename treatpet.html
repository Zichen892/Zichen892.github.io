Database design and implementation: Treat your Pets

Technical skills: MySQL, Python, MongoDB, Neo4j

The objective of this work is to provide a database management system for a new application called Treat Your Pet. This database system has three main achievements based on system performance, business analysis, and social networking by implementing the database on MySQL, MongoDB and Neo4j.
